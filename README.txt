Here scripts based on pywikibot used on wikipedia, wikicommons, wikidata, wiktionary:

* ogg_files_usage_on_wikt.py: this script creates a list of non used ogg files on related fr.wiktionary articles; .ogg files are stored on wikiCommons (January 2016)
** https://fr.wiktionary.org/w/index.php?title=Utilisateur:Beno%C3%AEt_Prieur/French_pronunciation (see history)

* diagram_creationdate_wikt.py: generates two diagrams representing the number of creation of thesaurus in French on fr.wiktionary since 2004, cumulative or not (January 2016)
** https://fr.wiktionary.org/w/index.php?title=Utilisateur:Beno%C3%AEt_Prieur/Th%C3%A9saurus_en_fran%C3%A7ais (see history)

* wikt_br_words_not_existing_on_fr: br words existing on br.wiktionary and not on fr.wiktionary  (January 2016)
** https://fr.wiktionary.org/wiki/Utilisateur:Beno%C3%AEt_Prieur/Comparaison_fr_br (see history)

* diagram_size_time.py: global size (in octets) of thesaurus in French on fr.wiktionary, month by month since 2004  (January 2016)
** https://fr.wiktionary.org/wiki/Utilisateur:Beno%C3%AEt_Prieur/SizeTime_Th%C3%A9saurus_en_fran%C3%A7ais (see history)
